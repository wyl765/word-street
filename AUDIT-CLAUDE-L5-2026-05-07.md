## 一、定义错误与不准确

### 1. 定义错误

| 文件 | 单词 | 问题 |
|------|------|------|
| 5a | `implicit` | 定义 "suggested but not straight stated" → "straight" 应为 "directly" |
| 5a | `indispensable` | 定义 "fully needed; cannot do with no" → 语法破碎，应为 "absolutely necessary; cannot do without" |
| 5a | `respective` | 定义 "belonging apart to each one mentioned" → "apart" 应为 "separately"，语法不通 |
| 5b | `disseminate` | 定义 "to spread info widely" → "info" 是缩写/口语，不应出现在正式定义中 |
| 5b | `bureaucrat` | 定义 "a state official who follows rigid rules" → 过度简化且带贬义偏见；bureaucrat 指政府行政人员，不一定 "rigid" |
| 5c | `circa` | 定义 "about or about, used with dates" → 重复 "about"，明显笔误，应为 "approximately, used with dates" |
| 5c | `clarification` | 定义 "a reason that makes something easier to get" → "reason" 应为 "explanation"，"get" 应为 "understand" |
| 5c | `envoy` | 定义 "a messenger or person who speaks for sent on a mission" → 语法破碎，缺失宾语 |
| 5c | `genealogy` | 定义 "the study of family the past and ancestors" → "family the past" 语法错误，应为 "family history and ancestors" |
| 5c | `exposition` | 定义 "a clear reason" → 应为 "a clear explanation" |
| 5c | `encryption` | 定义 "converting facts into a secret code for a shield" → "facts" 应为 "data/information"，"a shield" 应为 "security/protection" |
| 5c | `bibliography` | 定义中 "each write up" → 应为 "each report" 或 "each paper" |
| 5c | `internship` | 定义 "a period of work feel for a student" → "feel" 是错字，应为 "experience" |
| 5d | `redundant` | 例句 "Saying 'gift' is redundant because all gifts are free" → 逻辑错误。"free gift" 中 "free" 是 redundant，不是 "gift"。且定义例句应为 "Saying 'free gift' is redundant…" |
| 5d | `reprimand` | 定义 "a formal look of disapproval" → "look" 应为 "expression" 或直接说 "a formal statement of disapproval" |
| 5d | `emissary` | 定义 "a person sent on a special mission as a person who speaks for" → 语法不完整，缺失宾语 |
| 5d | `jurisprudence` | 例句 "The teacher taught jurisprudence at the school law school" → "school law school" 明显错误/冗余 |

### 2. 定义对10岁学生过于复杂或使用了生僻释义

| 文件 | 单词 | 问题 |
|------|------|------|
| 5a | `pursuant` | "done in accordance with a rule" — 10岁学生不太会遇到这个法律用语，且定义本身也用了生词 "in accordance with" |
| 5a | `statutory` | 法律术语对10岁偏难，但勉强可接受 |
| 5b | `mercantile` | 对10岁学生极为生僻 |
| 5b | `geopolitical` | 对10岁学生极为生僻 |
| 5b | `collateral` | 金融概念，定义对10岁学生过难 |
| 5d | `jurisprudence` | 对10岁学生极为生僻 |

## 二、例句问题

| 文件 | 单词 | 问题 |
|------|------|------|
| 5a | `administer` | "while the boss is away" — 学校场景用 "boss" 不恰当，应为 "vice principal" 或 "director" |
| 5a | `agonize` | "which dress to wear" — 性别刻板暗示（he + dress），虽非错误但值得注意 |
| 5a | `capitalism` | 定义过度简化为 "people own their own businesses"，忽略了私有制和市场机制的核心 |
| 5b | `albeit` | 例句无法帮助10岁学生理解这个连词的用法 |
| 5b | `fidelity` | 定义 "being true and loyal; it can also mean how close a copy is to the real thing" — 前半部分语法awkward |
| 5b | `repertoire` | 例句 "pop jazz, and classical" 缺少逗号 → 应为 "pop, jazz, and classical" |
| 5c | `sabotage` | 定义 "to on purpose destroy" → 语序错误，应为 "to deliberately/purposely destroy" |
| 5c | `cardiovascular` | 例句 "cardiovascular work out" → 应为 "cardiovascular workout"（一个词） |
| 5c | `cliche` | 单词缺少重音符 → 应为 `cliché`（例句中正确拼为 cliché，但 word 字段没有） |
| 5c | `lethargy` | 定义 "a state of having very little power or enthusiasm" → "power" 应为 "energy" |
| 5d | `ambivalent` | 例句 "excited also sad" → 缺连词，应为 "excited but also sad" |
| 5d | `denouncement` | 该词的标准形式是 `denunciation`，`denouncement` 极少使用且部分词典不收录 |

## 九、结构/格式问题

1. **5c 文件有格式不一致**：`conglomerate` 前有空行（多余的换行），打断了数组格式的一致性。
2. **5d 文件**：`credulous` 后也有空行问题，同上。
3. **5d 文件**：`debacle` 前有空行问题。
4. **部分例句标点**：5b `repertoire` 缺逗号。
5. **word 字段**：`gross domestic product` 作为词条不合理——这是一个短语，不是单词。作为 Level 5 词汇卡片，应考虑用 `GDP` 或删除。
6. **`cliche` vs `cliché`**：word 字段缺少重音符号。
