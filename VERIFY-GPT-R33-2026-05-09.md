# VERIFY-GPT-R33 — 正方（攻击者/家长）报告

**角色**: 被这本词典害惨的家长。我儿子用了这本词典考试全错。每找到一个错误律师费报销一万。
**范围**: words-level1.js 前200词
**日期**: 2026-05-09

---

## Finding 1: "bridge" — 定义错误（structure ≠ road）
- **词条**: bridge
- **问题**: 定义为 "a road over water or a valley"。Bridge不是road。Merriam-Webster定义为"a structure carrying a pathway or roadway over a depression or obstacle"。桥是一个structure（结构），不是road本身。我儿子考试写"bridge is a type of road"被扣分。
- **测试用例**: 问学生"Is a bridge a road?"，如果回答"yes"则证明定义有误导性。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "a structure carrying a pathway or roadway over a depression or obstacle (such as a river)"。Cambridge: "a structure that is built over a river, road, or railway to allow people and vehicles to cross from one side to the other."

## Finding 2: "lemon" — 用词不当（sour比sharp更准确且更简单）
- **词条**: lemon
- **问题**: 定义为 "a yellow fruit that tastes sharp and not sweet"。"Sharp"描述味道在英语中不如"sour"常见，且"sour"是L1词更简单。对于10岁ESL学生，"sharp"可能联想到knife/edge，产生混淆。
- **测试用例**: 问学生"What does a lemon taste like?"，如果说"sharp like a knife"而不是"sour"则证明定义措辞不当。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "the acid fruit of..."; Cambridge: "an oval fruit that has a thick, yellow skin and sour juice"; Oxford Learner's: "a yellow citrus fruit with a lot of sour juice"。三大词典都用sour，没有一个用sharp。

## Finding 3: "vest" — 定义不准确（vest ≠ jacket）
- **词条**: vest
- **问题**: 定义为 "a jacket with no sleeves"。Jacket暗示外套/outerwear，但vest通常是穿在shirt之上、jacket之下的中间层garment。Merriam-Webster定义不含jacket一词。
- **测试用例**: 给学生看西装三件套图片，问"which one is the vest?"，如果指着外套（因为定义说jacket）则证明定义有误导。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "a sleeveless garment for the upper body usually worn over a shirt"; Cambridge: "a piece of underwear worn under a shirt, etc. next to the skin"（英式）或"a piece of clothing without sleeves that is worn over a shirt"（美式）。

## Finding 4: "cottage" — 定义含主观词（pretty）
- **词条**: cottage
- **问题**: 定义为 "a small pretty house"。"Pretty"是主观形容词，不属于定义。一个破旧的cottage也是cottage。这不是词典应有的措辞。
- **测试用例**: 给学生看一个破旧的cottage图片，学生会说"this is not a cottage because it's not pretty"。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "the dwelling of a farm laborer or small farmer"; Cambridge: "a small house, usually in the countryside"; Oxford: "a small house, especially in the country"。没有任何权威词典在定义中使用pretty。

## Finding 5: "desert" — 定义冗余
- **词条**: desert
- **问题**: 定义为 "a very dry place with very little rain"。"Very dry"和"very little rain"表达同一个意思，是冗余。对于L1 ESL学生，冗余不一定是问题，但更简洁的定义更好。
- **测试用例**: 让学生解释desert，如果只说"dry place"或只说"place with little rain"，证明冗余部分无信息增量。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "arid land with usually sparse vegetation"; Cambridge: "an area, often covered with sand or rocks, where there is very little rain and not many plants"。MW用一个概念（arid），不做冗余重复。

## Finding 6: "tunnel" — 定义过窄
- **词条**: tunnel
- **问题**: 定义为 "a road that goes through a mountain"。1) Tunnel不是road，是passage/passageway。2) Tunnel不只穿过mountain，也穿过地下（subway tunnel）、水底（Channel Tunnel）等。
- **测试用例**: 问学生"The subway goes through a tunnel under the city. Is that a tunnel?"如果学生说"no, because it's not through a mountain"则证明定义过窄。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "a covered passageway"; Cambridge: "a long passage under or through the ground, especially one that is made by people"。

## Finding 7: "collar" — 定义遗漏常见含义
- **词条**: collar
- **问题**: 定义为 "the part of a shirt around your neck"。但对10岁孩子来说，dog collar（狗项圈）是更常见的接触场景。定义只给了衣领义。虽然QA标准说L1-L2单一词义，但选的不是高频义。
- **测试用例**: 给学生看狗戴collar的图片，问"what is this?"，如果学生说"it's not a collar because collar is part of a shirt"则证明选义问题。
- **外部证据**: COCA频率数据中"dog collar"和"shirt collar"都是高频搭配。Merriam-Webster第一义项是"a band, strip, or chain worn around the neck"，不限于衣服。

## Finding 8: "coconut" — 分类不准确
- **词条**: coconut
- **问题**: 定义为 "a big brown fruit with white inside"。椰子在植物学上是drupe（核果），不是typical fruit。但更大的问题是：椰子外壳不是brown——未剥壳的椰子是green。只有去了外壳的才是brown。
- **测试用例**: 给学生看超市里带绿壳的椰子，学生会说"this is not a coconut because it's not brown"。
- **外部证据**: Merriam-Webster: "the large ovoid fruit of the coconut palm whose outer fibrous husk yields coir"。椰子的外层（husk）是绿色的。
