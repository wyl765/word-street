## 4. Cross-Level Check

- **nevertheless/nonetheless overlap:** Both in level2b, similar meaning. Acceptable — they are distinct transition words kids encounter.
- **"as a result" vs "consequently" vs "because of" vs "due to":** Four near-synonyms all in level2b. Definitions are distinct enough ("because of that" vs "as a result of that" vs "caused by; as a result of" vs "because of"). No circular dependency.
- **"in general" vs "generally" vs "by and large" vs "on the whole" vs "overall":** Five near-synonyms. Defs differ enough.
- **"end up" vs "wind up":** Both mean to finish in a situation. Defs are different ("to finally be in a place or situation" vs "to end up in a place or situation"). MINOR: wind up def references "end up" concept. Acceptable since both are in same file.
- **Level dependency check:** "bacteria" in germ def is ok — both are in this file. "organ" referenced by brain/lung/heart defs — organ is also in this file. No upward dependencies.
