## MAJOR

### words-level4b.js — reverberate
- **词：** reverberate
- **原文：**
  - definition: `to be repeated as an echo have lasting effects`
  - example: `The thunder reverberated through the valley and shook the cabin windows.`
- **问题：** definition 语法错误且义项并列缺少连接词（应为 “echo, and/or have lasting effects”）。对10岁学习者不友好，容易误解。
- **建议修改：**
  - definition（更清晰、同义项对齐）：`to echo, or to have effects that continue for a long time`
  - 或更简洁：`to echo; to have lasting effects`

### words-level3c.js — inundate
- **词：** inundate
- **原文：**
  - example: `After the story went viral the reporter was inundated with phone calls.`
- **问题：** 例句缺少引导性从句后的逗号（After…），属于明显标点错误。
- **建议修改：** `After the story went viral, the reporter was inundated with phone calls.`

### words-level4a.js — inevitable
- **词：** inevitable
- **原文：**
  - example: `After eating three slices of cake a stomachache felt inevitable.`
- **问题：**
  1) 缺少引导性从句后的逗号（After…）。
  2) “a stomachache felt inevitable” 搭配不自然（主语 stomachache 不会 “feel inevitable”）；应改为 “was/seemed inevitable”。
- **建议修改：** `After eating three slices of cake, a stomachache seemed inevitable.`
  - 更简单：`After eating three slices of cake, a stomachache was inevitable.`

### words-level4b.js — monopoly

- **原文：**
  - example: `When one company has a monopoly customers have no other choices and prices often rise.`
- **问题：**
  - 缺少引导性从句后的逗号（When…）。
  - 句子偏长，多个分句挤在一起；对目标年龄段读者可读性较差。
- **建议修改（推荐拆句）：**
  - `When one company has a monopoly, customers have no other choices. Prices often rise.`
  - 或不拆句：`When one company has a monopoly, customers have no other choices, and prices often rise.`

## 建议固化项

- 🔧 [proofcheck规则] **检测句首引导成分缺少逗号**：例句以 `After / Before / When / While / Because / If / Once / Instead of` 等开头时，若在前~60字符内未出现逗号，应提示“introductory clause needs a comma”。
- 🔧 [proofcheck规则] **例句包含目标词的变形匹配增强**：处理辅音双写/加 k 的过去式与分词（如 `char→charred`, `allot→allotted`, `extol→extolled`, `dub→dubbed`, `frolic→frolicked`），避免误报“例句未包含目标词”。
- 🔧 [标准更新] QA-STANDARD.md：补充/强调“句首从属结构或介词短语通常需要逗号（introductory comma）”，并建议对10岁读者优先使用短句，避免多分句堆叠与破折号长句。
